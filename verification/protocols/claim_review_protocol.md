# Claim Review Protocol

## Minimum Review

Each claim requires:

- Claim ID
- Exact wording under review
- Source or sources checked
- Verification status
- Confidence level
- Reviewer note
- Date reviewed

## Reviewer Questions

1. Is the claim factual, analytical, hypothetical, or speculative?
2. Does the source directly support the wording?
3. Is the source primary, contemporary, secondary, or internal?
4. Are there conflicts with other sources?
5. Does the claim involve living people, reputational harm, or sensitive personal material?
6. What wording would be more precise?

## Promotion Criteria

A claim can be promoted into manuscript prose only when it is verified, partly verified with limits stated, or explicitly presented as a hypothesis or unresolved question.
