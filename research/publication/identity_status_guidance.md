# Identity Status Guidance

This file is mandatory wording guidance for all future manuscript chapters.

## Current Controlled Status

- Preferred short wording: `researcher/lab-led Carl Charles Webb attribution, pending official coroner/SAPOL determination`.
- Research attribution: Professor Derek Abbott, Colleen Fitzpatrick, Astrea Forensics and ABC reporting support a serious DNA/genealogy attribution to Carl Charles Webb.
- Official police position: current public reporting still requires caution; the project has not acquired a final SAPOL/FSSA report.

- Government statements: the 2021 exhumation was official; a final public identification record is still an intelligence gap in this repository.
- Research consensus: strong public research lead, not a project-verified legal identity.

## Approved Wording

- `Carl Charles Webb attribution`
- `researcher/lab-led identification lead`
- `Webb attribution pending official confirmation`
- `the unidentified man, if the Webb attribution is officially confirmed`
- `Mission 11 OSINT materially strengthens the Webb lead but does not close the official-status gap`

## Deprecated Wording

- `the Somerton Man was Carl Webb`
- `identity solved`
- `officially identified as Carl Webb`
- `case closed`
- `DNA proved the identity` without naming the source class and official-status limit

## Required Chapter Handling

- Chapter 1 must state the official-status caveat wherever Webb appears in a key judgment.
- Chapter 4 may present Webb material in a conditional identity section.
- Chapter 8 may discuss Webb-related relationship leads only as conditional leads.
- Chapters 10 and 11 must not infer motive, cause or intelligence status from the Webb attribution alone.
