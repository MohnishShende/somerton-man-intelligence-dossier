2. What exact property and clothing items were recorded, and what chain of custody exists?
3. What is the strongest source basis for the identification as Carl Webb?
4. Which claims about Jessica Thomson are documented, and which derive from later retellings?
5. What exact dates apply to the Tamam Shud slip, the Rubaiyat copy, and any writing inside the book?
6. What handwriting samples exist for relevant persons, and are they suitable for comparison?
7. What accepted cryptographic analyses of the Rubaiyat writing exist?
8. What contemporary newspaper coverage exists, and did its details change over time?
9. Which intelligence-context claims are merely background, and which have case-specific evidence?
10. What forensic testing remains possible, if any evidence survives in usable condition?
