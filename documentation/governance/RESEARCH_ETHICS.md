# Research Ethics Statement

This project concerns a real death, real families, and people who may still have living relatives. The repository must avoid sensationalism, harassment, and unsupported accusations.

## Principles

- Treat the deceased and relatives with dignity.
- Avoid defamatory claims about named individuals.
- Do not publish private personal data without strong public-interest justification.
- Distinguish public records from private information.
- Preserve uncertainty.
- Correct errors promptly and transparently.

## Living People

Claims about living people require heightened care. Use only reliable public sources and avoid unnecessary personal detail.

## Speculation

Speculative scenarios may be retained for analytical comparison, but they must not be presented as findings.
